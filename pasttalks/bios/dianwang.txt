Dian Wang is a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at the Khoury College of Computer Science at Northeastern University, advised by Professor Robert Platt and Professor Robin Walters. His research interests include Robotic Manipulation, Equivariant Machine Learning, and Reinforcement Learning. His research focuses on applying equivariant learning methods in robotic manipulation to improve sample efficiency and generalization. Prior to his Ph.D., he received a Master's degree in Computer Science from Northeastern University and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Sichuan University.
