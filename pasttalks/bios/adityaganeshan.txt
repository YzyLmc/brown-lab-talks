Aditya Ganeshan is a second-year Ph.D. student at the Brown visual computing group, Brown University advised by Professor Daniel Ritchie. He is interested in developing techniques for data-driven inference of structured representations for data (typically 3D data). Previously, he worked on computer vision research at Preferred Networks Inc., Tokyo, Japan, and adversarial robustness of neural networks at IISc, Banglore, India . He completed his Bachelor’s & Master’s degree in Applied Mathematics from IIT Roorkee, India.
