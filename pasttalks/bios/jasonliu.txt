Jason Liu is a Ph.D. candidate at Brown University, advised by Prof. Stefanie Tellex. His work lies in the intersection of robotics and natural language processing. He is building robotic systems that understand natural language commands specifying long-horizon temporal tasks. Previously, Jason earned his Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences from UC Berkeley. He is fortunately being funded by NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program and Jack Kent Cooke Foundation Graduate Scholarship.
